## What is OpenPlanetData?

OpenPlanetData is an open and free initiative dedicated to making open data related to our planet Earth more accessible and efficient to use. We provide daily snapshots of geographic datasets in optimized formats, hosted on Cloudflare R2 for fast global access.

<CardGrid>
	<Card title="OpenStreetMap Snapshots" icon="globe">
		Daily snapshots of OpenStreetMap data in multiple formats including GOB and GOL files indexed with GeoDesk for rapid spatial queries.
	</Card>
	<Card title="Time Zone Data" icon="document">
		Global time zone boundary data updated daily, converted to GeoParquet format for efficient storage and fast spatial queries.
	</Card>
	<Card title="Open & Transparent" icon="open-book">
		All our data pipelines are open source. Know exactly how every dataset is built, validated, and maintained.
	</Card>
	<Card title="Production Ready" icon="rocket">
		Datasets are optimized for performance with formats like GeoParquet and GeoDesk-indexed files for rapid queries.
	</Card>
</CardGrid>
