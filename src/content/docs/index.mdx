## What is OpenPlanetData?

OpenPlanetData is an open and free initiative dedicated to making open data related to our planet Earth more accessible and efficient to use. We provide regular snapshots of geographic datasets in optimized formats, most updated daily, hosted on Cloudflare R2 for fast global access.
